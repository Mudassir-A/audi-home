R8 Spyder






Pure super sports car experience

A breathtaking experience awaits you in the Audi R8 Spyder- especially when the roof is down.
Feel and hear the power of the V10 mid-engine, which generates up to 620 PS
and has maximum torque of 580 Nm depending upon the model chosen.


The heart of the vehicle –
the V10 engine

The naturally aspirated V10 engine is located directly
behind the passenger compartment. Its 5.2-litre FSI
generates 570PS in the V10 Performance RWD or up to 620
PS in the V10 Performance quattro, which sprints from 0-
62mph in 3.2 seconds.

And thanks to the mid-engine concept with a low centre of
gravity, it has a balanced weight distribution, enabling high
speeds even when cornering. Plus, the power steering
provides intensive contact to the road..

Explore trim levels

he R8 Spyder is available in different versions, known as trim levels. These allow you to choose a version that reflects your preferred styling, driving experience and equipment level.


Emotion Design

It pays to drive the Audi R8 Spyder with the hood down. An
electrohydraulic drive folds the hood under a carbon-fibre-
reinforced polymer lid in just 20 seconds and at speeds of
up to 30 mph. It’s just as easy to retract and extend the rear
wind deflector at the push of a button.

Combine this with wheel arches that exude power, flattened
grooves over the Singleframe, the end-to-end air vent grille
at the rear, gloss black 19-inch alloy wheels as standard and
the oval exhaust tailpipes - and you’ve got one seriously
dynamic vehicle.

Exclusive and sporty
interior

In the Audi R8 Spyder, you take a seat in R8 Super Sports
seats with upholstery in fine Nappa leather. The standard
R8 3-spoke flat bottomed sports leather steering wheel
with multifunction plus and two control assistant programs
give you full control of the vehicle.

Upgrade to the V10 Performance RWD Edition for enhanced
interior specification, including carbon fibre upper inlays
surrounding the air vents, sporty diamond stitched front
seats and a high performance Bang and Olufsen sound
system.


Beauty and brains

When it comes to infotainment with the Audi R8 Spyder you
benefit from the 12.3 inch high resolution Audi Virtual
Cockpit, as well as the MMI Navigation plus with MMI touch.
The MMI touch interface recognises your handwriting: you
can input characters and scroll and zoom with your fingers.
Alternatively, you can control the MMI using the voice
control system.

The driver assistance technologies1 including Parking
System Plus front and rear, rear-view camera and Advanced
key all work to ensure each journey in the R8 Coupe is as
smooth as it is exhilarating.
