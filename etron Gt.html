e-tron GT quattro

Pure sportiness and progressive luxury

Audi e-tron GT quattro

Stopping Power:

Sporty detailing, such as wide wheel arches, a prominent
rear diffuser and detailed air intakes, signal a powerful
presence.

Active Aerodynamics:

The e-tron GT quattro cuts through air; a flowing profile
culminating in a rear spoiler that extends automatically
at higher speeds.

Functional, yet beautiful:

Large intakes flank the purposeful front bumper and
honeycombed, singleframe grille; even functional details
are striking.

Dramatically lit:

Digital LED headlights are standard - upgrade to
Vorsprung trim for Matrix LED headlights with Audi laser
light for a distinctive X-shaped design and light
‘signature’.

Leave an impression:

The rear LED light bar spans the entire width; arrows
converge into the strip, leaving a dramatic impression on
driving off.

Stunning design outside.

A sculpted profile. A low, wide stance. The e-tron GT quattro visibly marks the new era for Audi electric performance vehicles.

Thrilling design inside.

Inside, the e-tron GT quattro blends luxury, space and sportiness for all passengers. And for the driver, a sporty ‘monoposto’ cockpit design reminiscent of race track cars tilts instrument panels towards the driving position.

High performance display:

SThe full HD virtual cockpit displays information directly in
your field of view. Choose from “e-tron”, “sport” and
“classic” display options

Leather-free:

An optional pack replaces leather with different materials
including Dinamica, artificial leather and innovative
cascade cloth.

Panoramic Views:

With a panoramic glass roof that bathes the cabin in
natural light, you’re never short of dramatic views.

Seats of power:

Sport seats are standard; or upgrade to the optional 14
way adjustable Sports Seats plus which come as part of
the leather free pack

Finishing Touches:

Graphite grey inlays adorn dash and doors; upgrade to
sporty Carbon Twill, or, for a luxurious touch, a natural
walnut finish.
